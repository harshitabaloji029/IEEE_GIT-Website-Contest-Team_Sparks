About Execom

An executive committee has special responsibilities and authorities above all committees. Usually it can act on behalf of the full board. Its main purpose is to facilitate decision making between board meetings or in urgent and crisis circumstances. So for the academic year 2020-21 the responsibility is handed over to these excellent members who have worked continuously for IEEE.
